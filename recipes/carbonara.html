Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is eaten all over the world, often cooked wrong.

On this page you will find how Italians do it (even though they might be on disagreement on the type of cheese to use).

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Bacon cubes

Preparation

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Cook the bacon and mix the egg
  3. Add the cooked Pasta to the egg'n'bacon mix and stir until the typical Carbonara texture gets created
